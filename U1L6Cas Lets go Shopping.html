üåç Culture Corner

Explore shopping culture around the world!

üáØüáµ Japan - Convenience Stores Everywhere

In Japan, convenience stores (konbini) are on almost every corner. They sell everything from food to books. Shopping is quick and efficient, and stores are very clean. Many people shop there daily instead of large supermarkets.

üáÆüáπ Italy - The Piazza Market Tradition

In many Italian towns, outdoor markets (piazza) are the heart of shopping culture. People negotiate prices and chat with vendors. It's a social experience, not just buying things!

üáπüá≠ Thailand - Floating Markets

Traditional floating markets in Thailand are located on water! Vendors sell from boats, and shoppers navigate by boat. It's a unique cultural experience that's been around for centuries.

üá∫üá∏ USA - Black Friday & Cyber Monday

In the United States, Black Friday (day after Thanksgiving) and Cyber Monday are major shopping events with huge discounts. People wait in line early morning for the best deals!
